1. Log in to the Smart Meeting Management Platform as an administrator and open the Device Management module of Meeting Room Management. Click "+" in the meeting room area to add a new meeting room.

<img  src={useBaseUrl("img/doc-en/激活新增会议室-en.png")} />

2. After adding a conference room, click [Add activation Code] to select the corresponding conference room and click OK to generate the activation code.
